Property Features

Striking Craftsman style townhome in the incredibly convenient location of Mission Meridian Village, in the heart of the Mission West District. This New Urbanism village was designed by renowned architects Moule & Polyzoides, and this is an end unit, so there is a lot of light with windows on three sides. Details include shingle siding, wood casement and double-hung windows, dual zone AC, extra deep bath tubs, oil rubbed bronze details, stainless steel appliances, skylight, fireplace in the living room, and wired for alarm, high-speed internet and cable services. Third floor bedroom partition wall can be easily removed (currently used as two bedrooms).

The Metro Gold Line Mission Station is very close, the weekly Thursday Farmers Market, great restaurants, coffee shops, and community events. This is a mixed-use village with its own retail stores including a wonderful bakery, exercise studio and a bridal store.

South Pasadena is a friendly community, and its award winning schools have some of the highest API's in the state of California. Elementary: Arroyo Vista (API 932) Middle: South Pasadena Middle School (API 912) High School: South Pasadena High School (API 887). Your chance to live in beautiful South Pasadena is here!
